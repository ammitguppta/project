Welcome To Waterboom

WaterBoom and Amusement Park

Discover an exciting new world at WaterBoom and Amusement Park ... For people seeking a great holiday retreat that also offers a glimpse into the rich traditional beach culture, WaterBoom and Amusement Park is just perfect.

Spread over beautifully landscaped coconut gardens on the west coast of Mumbai, this luxurious beach resort nestles on the soft white sands of Navapur Beach situated about 7 kms from the Virar and 15 kms from the Vasai Railway Station.
